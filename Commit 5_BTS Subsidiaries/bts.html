WHAT IS BTS?


BTS, an acronym of Bangtan Sonyeondan or “Beyond the Scene,” is a Grammy-nominated South Korean group that has been capturing the hearts of millions of fans through their exceptional music and heart-touching lyrics since its debut in June 13, 2013.

BTS advocates their "Love Yourself" campaign in order for youth to live their Most Beautiful Moment In Life
