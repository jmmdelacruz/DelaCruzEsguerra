BIGHIT MUSIC is an industry-leading music label that continuously strives to pioneer new paths since 2005. BIGHIT MUSIC and their artists are driven by their strong belief in music and everlasting support from their fans and supporters. K-Pop boy groups under the label includes BTS and TOMORROW X TOGETHER.






BELIFT LAB is a label which was established jointly by CJ ENM and HYBE in 2018. BELIFT LAB was made with a goal of flourishing idol groups while making an impact on changing the label business. An artist under this label is ENHYPEN which is a K-pop boyband formed in 2020.





PLEDIS is a major record label & comprehensive entertainment agency in South Korea that was established in 2007. The label name, PLEDIS, was inspired by the star cluster, Pleiades. PLEDIS aims to cultivate K-Pop idol stars-in-training to be luminous stars of K-Pop. Seventeen is PLEDIS’s one and only star K-Pop boy-group which was formed in 2015.
